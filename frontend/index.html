About Us

Welcome to eagless View Kungwini

Located in Bronkhorstspruit, Gauteng, and just a short walk from Bronkhorstspruit Dam, eagless View Kungwini offers a tranquil escape for families and couples.

Our 7 comfortable units feature en-suite bathrooms, a shared kitchen, lounge, patio, and access to amenities like a pool, braai facilities, Wi-Fi, and secure parking.

Enjoy nearby activities such as bird watching, fishing, cycling, and horseback riding. Whether you’re seeking relaxation or adventure, eagless View Kungwini is your perfect getaway.

Let us take care of your every need and make your stay truly unforgettable.
